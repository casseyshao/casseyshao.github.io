About

Hello, my name is Cassey! I am an undergraduate student at University of Toronto, pursuing a major in computer engineering, a minor in business, and a certificate in artificial intelligence. I am currently on my program's Professional Experience Year (PEY) Co-op program, where I am interning as a junior software developer at Edsembli Inc. On this page, I share my projects, interests, and more! Thanks so much for visiting!

Projects and Labs

  • Cat and Mouse Game (C)

    Worked with a partner to build a game, where the user is a mouse who needs to avoid a cat and travel through a maze to reach a block of cheese. Used the DE1-SoC board for user inputs and to display game information.

  • Maps Application (C++)

    Worked in a team of 3 to build a mapping program. Used EZGL (drawing package) and GTK to visualize the map UI. Used/extended an API querying OpenStreetMap database to obtain and display geographic information.

  • Text Conferencing (C)

    Worked with a partner to implement a text conferencing application, where a conference is a list of registered users (clients) that are sending text messages to each other. The server and client processes were implemented.

Other Interests

  • Flying

    I work part-time as a familiarization glider pilot for the Royal Canadian Air Cadet Program. In this role, I take cadets up for glider flights to introduce them to gliding, and hopefully inspire them to pursue their glider/private pilot licenses through the program.

  • School Community

    I love being involved in my community and make time for it outside of my courses. I was a head leader for engineering orientation week, an executive for two years with Sustainable Engineers Association, and I played some games with the intramural girls’ hockey team.

  • Outdoor Activies

    I am always excited to be outside and active! I worked as a snowboard instructor for the City of Toronto and played sports–such as ice hockey and soccer–growing up! I also really enjoy hiking, biking, swimming, and joining drop-in fitness classes at my school’s gym!
